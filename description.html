BIZNEZ Description BIZNEZ is an incredible new app that will change the way you maximize your business school experience. Have too many events to keep track of and attend? Fear not! BIZNEZ will keep track of all business-school related events, be it clubs, careers or simply social events organized by your business school friends. Prioritize types of events that you are interested in to ensure you are notified of events that you need to register for, easily register and pay on the app, and keep track of events that you have registered for as well as those that your friends are going to. Coming to you on November 1st.
